Fraud Detection in UPI

Developed a data-driven fraud detection model for UPI payment transactions. Applied dimensionality reduction using PCA and trained logistic regression to identify suspicious behavior patterns based on transaction volume and value trends. Focused on improving early anomaly detection without explicit fraud labels. Tech Used: Python, pandas, scikit-learn, matplotlib Outcome: Enabled effective flagging of potential fraudulent patterns in monthly customer-initiated UPI transactions.
